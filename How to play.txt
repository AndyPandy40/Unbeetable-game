How to play:
1. Press 1 or click on the tower in the show to buy a tower (100 coins)
2. Right click on an already placed tower to upgrade it (300 coins)
3. Press p to pause
4. Press esc to cancel buying a tower

Tips:
1. You can place towers on the path so long as they don't block the pathing of the bees
2. Try to place the towers in a zig zag pattern to make the bees take the longest path possible
